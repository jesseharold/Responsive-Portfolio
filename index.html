About Me

I grew up on St. Croix in the U.S. Virgin Islands. I lived in New York City for the past 15 years, and just moved to Los Angeles this past May. Culture shock! Where are all the good bagels? Why must I drive?

I have worked as a web developer for a few years. I also draw comics, make puppets, and am currently working on a stop-motion animation for a Halloween video show. I hope that I can work my two jobs, finish my animation, and keep up with my coding boot camp homework!
